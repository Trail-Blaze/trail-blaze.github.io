What Is a Private Server?


A Fortnite Private Server (or a "dev server" as the internet may call it) is a software which emulates the Epic Games' Fortnite database. It allows you to view and access certain items or cosmetics which would only be available to you through purchasing the items. Fortnite Private Servers do NOT connect to the initial database otherwise known as *-prod.ol.epicgames.com. A private server also has the capability to customize and edit announcements, the shop, locker items, skins/cosmetics, gifting capability, etc.

Some even have the capability to work in-game

Is This Even Legal?


In short: Yes, it is

Although the topic is pretty controversal, most people claim that it is in fact legal as long as you are not interfering with Epic Games' operations. But if in fact hosting and distributing a private server is illegal, as soon as Blaze receives a letter such as a Cease and Desist letter from Epic Games' lawyer, we'll be sure to comply and shut down our operations.

Either way, you'll always be in the clear. It's us who will be liable
